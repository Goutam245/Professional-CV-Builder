CV Writing Tips

Make your CV stand out with these professional tips

01

Keep it concise

Aim for a one-page CV that highlights your most relevant experience and skills.

02

Tailor to the job

Customize your CV for each position by emphasizing relevant skills and experience.

03

Use action verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "developed," "implemented," or "optimized."

04

Quantify achievements

Include numbers and metrics to demonstrate the impact of your work.

05

Proofread carefully

Eliminate typos and grammatical errors that can make a poor impression.

06

Include relevant projects

Showcase projects that demonstrate your technical skills and problem-solving abilities.
